About

Hi, my Vietnamese name is Bùi Nhật Tân. I'm currently working as a Research Assistant at Software Engineering Lab (SELab) - Ho Chi Minh University of Science under supervisor of Prof. Minh-Triet Tran, Prof. Ngan Le and Dr. Ngoc-Thao Nguyen. I received my bachelor degree in Computer & Information Science at the collaboration program between Ho Chi Minh University of Science and Auckland University of Technology. My CV can be found here.

My research concentrates on Deep Learning, Computer Vision and Image Processing. Particularly, I am interested in Multimodal Medical Image, Medical Image Analysis, Neural Style Transfer, etc.
