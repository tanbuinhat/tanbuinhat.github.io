About

Hi, my name is Bui Nhat Tan in Vietnamese. I'm currently working as a research assistant at Software Engineering Lab (SELab) - Ho Chi Minh University of Science under supervisor of Prof. Minh-Triet Tran and Dr. Ngoc-Thao Nguyen. I received my bachelor degree in Computer & Information Science at the collaboration program between Ho Chi Minh University of Science and Auckland University of Technology. My CV can be found here.

My research concentrates on Computer Vision and Image Processing, specially Deep Generative Models and how to apply them to re-create the works of art. Particularly, I am interested in Neural Style Transfer, Visual Sentiment Analysis, Medical Image Segmentation, etc.

I am looking for PhD opportunities in Computer Science and Computer Vision field.
